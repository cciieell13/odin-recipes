Scrambled Eggs

Fluffy eggs

One of the most simple yet flexible side dishes.

You can add other ingredients to the recipe but in this case I'll add what I usually put.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • A pinch of salt
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Turn on the stove to medium heat
  2. Add some oil on the frying pan and wait for it to heat.
  3. Beat the eggs and onto the frying pan and mix the teaspoon of salt.
  4. Keep mixing until the liquid is gone but the eggs are not dry.
