Chicken Adobo

Ingredients

  • 1 kilogram chicken cut ups
  • 2 tbsp canola oil
  • 6 cloves garlic crushed
  • 1 pc onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 pcs bay leaves
  • 1 tsp whole black peppercorns, slightly crushed
  • 2 pc knorr chicken cubes
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • 1 cup of spinach

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and saut√© garlic and onions. Add the chicken and sear it on all sides until the skin starts to brown.
  2. Pour in the vinegar, soy sauce, and water. Add the bay leaves, pepper, and Knorr Chicken Cubes.
  3. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce heat to medium-low. Do not cover the pan. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Remove the chicken pieces and fry in another pan until nicely browned.
  5. Place the fried chicken pieces back into the first pan. Add the spinach and sugar and let simmer for another 10 minutes or until the sauce has thickened. Serve warm.
